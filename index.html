Our Mission

FitPlan Pro was built with one goal: to make professional-level fitness and nutrition planning accessible to everyone. Most personalized plans cost hundreds of dollars from a nutritionist or coach. We believe that knowledge shouldn't be gated behind a paywall.

Whether you want to lose weight, build muscle, tone your body, or simply maintain a healthy lifestyle — FitPlan Pro generates a complete, science-backed program tailored to your exact body and goals in seconds.

How It Works

You enter your stats — age, height, weight, goal, activity level, training days, and food preferences. Our engine calculates your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) using the Mifflin-St Jeor formula, then multiplies it by your activity factor to find your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).

From there, we apply a scientifically appropriate caloric surplus or deficit based on your goal, distribute macronutrients (protein, carbohydrates, fats) optimally, and generate a full meal plan and workout schedule adapted to your preferences.
